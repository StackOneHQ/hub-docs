<Warning>
  Ensure you have Admin privileges for your Factorial HR account.
</Warning>

If you've been directed to StackOne to integrate with Factorial HR, the following steps will help you understand the process and any necessary actions to configure successful integration.

## Step 1: Create your OAuth Application

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to OAuth Applications page">
    Go to the [OAuth applications page](https://api.factorialhr.com). Click on the green button which says New Application.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="New Application" src="/images/factorialhr/image1.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Application Details">
    Enter the new Application's Name and enter the Redirect URI: `https://api.stackone.com/connect/oauth2/factorialhr/callback`. Then click the blue Save button.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Application Details" src="/images/factorialhr/image2.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 2: Get Your Client ID and Client Secret

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select an Existing Application">
    From the [OAuth applications page](https://api.factorialhr.com) click on an existing application. This will take you to the application's page.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Existing Application" src="/images/factorialhr/image3.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Copy Client ID and Client Secret">
    Here you will find your Client ID and Client Secret.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Client ID and Client Secret" src="/images/factorialhr/image4.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 3: Connecting with StackOne

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Integrations">
    Navigate to the Integrations page in the [StackOne Hub](https://app.stackone.com). Click on the Factorial HR tile under HRIS.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Authentication Type">
    Select OAuth2 under authentication type and Production under Environment type. Enter the Client ID and Client Secret from the previous step, then click the Save Integration button.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Save Integration" src="/images/factorialhr/image5.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Link Account">
    Navigate to the Accounts page. Select the black Link Account on the right and then select Factorial HR from the modal.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Link Account" src="/images/factorialhr/image6.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect">
    Select Connect.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Connect" src="/images/factorialhr/image7.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connection Successful">
    If successful, you will see a Connection Successful message. Your account is now linked!
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Connection Successful" src="/images/factorialhr/image8.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>
