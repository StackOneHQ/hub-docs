If you've been directed to StackOne to integrate with Factorial HR, the following steps will help you understand the process and any necessary actions to configure successful integration.

## Connecting with StackOne

<Steps>
  <Step title="Proceed to the Accounts page">
    Proceed to the Accounts page to establish a connection between FactorialHr and StackOne. Then click the `Connect` button.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Connect Button" src="/images/factorialhr/image1.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter your Factorial Hr account credentials">
    Enter your Factorial Hr account credentials in the opened tab.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Factorial Hr Account Credentials" src="/images/factorialhr/image2.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Sign In">
    Click sign in.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Click Sign In" src="/images/factorialhr/image3.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

You'll get an instant "`Account connected`" success message and the opened tab will close.
