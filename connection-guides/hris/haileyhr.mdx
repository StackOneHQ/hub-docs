<Warning>
  Ensure you have Admin privileges for your Hailey HR account.
</Warning>

If you've been directed to StackOne to integrate with Hailey HR, the following steps will help you understand the process and any necessary actions to configure successful integration.

## Generating API Key

<Steps>
  <Step title="Login to Hailey dashboard">
    Login to your Hailey dashboard. Click on your avatar in the upper-right corner and click Access tokens.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Hailey dashboard" src="/images/hailey-hr/image1.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Assign a name and generate token">
    Assign a name to your access token and click Generate.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Generate token" src="/images/hailey-hr/image2.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Copy your token">
    Copy your newly generated token to a safe place.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Copy token" src="/images/hailey-hr/image3.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Establish a connection with StackOne

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter API token">
    Upon reaching the Link Account page, enter your API token and proceed by clicking the `Connect` button.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Link Account" src="/images/hailey-hr/image4.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>
