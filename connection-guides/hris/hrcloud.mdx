If you've been directed to StackOne to integrate with HR Cloud, the following steps will help you understand the process and any necessary actions to configure successful integration.

## Get your Customer Key and Customer Secret

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in to HR Cloud">
    Sign in to the [HR Cloud website](https://corehr.hrcloud.com) and click on the Settings tab on the left-hand side menu.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Settings Tab" src="/images/hrcloud/image1.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Locate Service Accounts">
    In the Settings tab, locate the Global Settings section and click on the Service Accounts option.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Service Accounts" src="/images/hrcloud/image2.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create a new Service Account">
    After clicking on Service Accounts, a side menu will open. Here, you can check existing Service Accounts and create new ones. Click on the Add ➕ icon to create a new service account.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Add Service Account" src="/images/hrcloud/image3.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Service Account Details">
    Enter your Service Name. Enable the Service Account Status ☑️ to activate your service account. Click the Save button to complete the process.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Service Account Details" src="/images/hrcloud/image4.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Copy Customer Key and Customer Secret">
    Now, you can see your Customer Key and Customer Secret here in the list. Select and copy the Customer Key and Customer Secret to a safe place.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Customer Key and Customer Secret" src="/images/hrcloud/image5.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Use the API Key in the StackOne Hub

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter Customer Key and Customer Secret">
    Upon reaching the Link Account page, enter the Customer Key and Customer Secret and proceed by clicking the `Connect` button.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Link Account" src="/images/hrcloud/image6.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>


## Linking your Account

Enter the required fields from the previous steps in your connection form.

- Employment
- Groups
- Time Off
