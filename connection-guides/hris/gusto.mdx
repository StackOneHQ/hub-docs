  Ensure you have Admin Payroll Role privileges for your Gusto account.
</Warning>

If you've been directed to StackOne to integrate with Gusto, the following steps will help you understand the process and any necessary actions to configure successful integration.

## Establish a connection with StackOne

<Steps>
  <Step title="Proceed to the Accounts page">
    Proceed to the Accounts page to establish a connection between Gusto and StackOne. Then click the `Connect` button.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Connect Button" src="/images/gusto/image1.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter your Gusto account credentials">
    Enter your Gusto account credentials in the opened tab.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Gusto Account Credentials" src="/images/gusto/image2.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select your Company">
    Select your Company from the drop-down list, and click Authorize.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Authorize Company" src="/images/gusto/image3.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

You'll get an instant "`Account connected`" success message and the opened tab will close.
