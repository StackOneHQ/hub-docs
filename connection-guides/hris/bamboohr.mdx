If you've been directed to StackOne to integrate with BambooHR, the following steps will help you understand the process and any necessary actions to configure successful integration.

## Step 1: Getting your Company Domain

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in to BambooHR">
    Sign in to your BambooHR instance and take note of your company domain as the URL's prefix. In the example below, the Company Domain is `stackonetest`.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Company Domain" src="/images/bamboohr/image1.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 2: Generating your API Key

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to API Keys">
    On the Home page, click on your Account avatar and select API Keys.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="API Keys" src="/images/bamboohr/image2.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add New Key">
    Click Add New Key.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Add New Key" src="/images/bamboohr/image3.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter a name">
    Enter a name for the API key.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Enter a name" src="/images/bamboohr/image4.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Generate Key">
    Click Generate Key.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Copy the Key">
    Click COPY KEY to copy the key to your clipboard (and then click Done).
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Copy the Key" src="/images/bamboohr/image5.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 3: Connecting to StackOne

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter API Key and Company Domain">
    Upon reaching the Link Account page, enter the API Key and Company Domain details and proceed by clicking the `Connect` button.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Link Account" src="/images/bamboohr/image6.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

Congratulations, you're all set! If you face any issues with the steps mentioned above, please contact us via the live chat button or by emailing integrations@stackone.com. We're always here to assist you!

## OpenID SSO Authentication

BambooHR provides Client ID and Client Secret additional details for successful integration. Refer to [Single Sign-On (SSO) with OpenID Connect Bamboo doc](https://documentation.bamboohr.com/docs/single-sign-on-sso-with-openid-connect) for more information.

For SSO authentication, StackOne Hub requires specifying only your Company Domain from step 1.
