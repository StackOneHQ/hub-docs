If you've been directed to StackOne to integrate with Deel, the following steps will help you understand the process and any necessary actions to configure a successful integration.

## Log in to Deel

<Steps>
    <Step title="Connect to Deel">
        Click the Connect button to start the login process. You will be redirected to Deel.
    
        <Frame>
            <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Connect to Deel" src="/images/deel/image6.png" />
        </Frame>
    </Step>
    <Step title="Enter Credentials">
        If necessary, enter your Deel credentials or use one of the sign-in options available that are connected to your Deel account.
    
        <Frame>
            <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Sign in to Deel" src="/images/deel/image7.png" />
        </Frame>
    </Step>
    <Step title="Accept the required scopes">
        In order to connect to your Deel account, you need to grant permission for the App to access your data.
        
        Click **Allow** to proceed.
    
        <Frame>
            <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Accept permissions" src="/images/deel/image8.png" />
        </Frame>
    </Step>
</Steps>


## Linking your Account

Enter the required fields from the previous steps in your connection form.
