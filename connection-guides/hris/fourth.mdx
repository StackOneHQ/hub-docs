If you've been directed to StackOne to integrate with Fourth, the following steps will help you understand the process and any necessary actions to configure a successful integration.

## Getting the Credentials

<Steps>
    Ensure you have a `UK Employee API` account and your `Organization ID` to connect with Fourth.<br/>
    Contact the Fourth Professional Services team if you need help obtaining these credentials.<br/>
    You can find more information or request access on the <a href="https://developer.fourth.com/en-gb/uk-employee-api" target="_blank" rel="noopener">UK Employee API page</a>.
</Steps>

## Connecting with StackOne

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter the Details">
    Upon reaching the Link Account page, enter the `Username`, `Password`, and `Organization ID`.<br/>
    Then proceed by clicking the **Connect** button.

      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Link Account" src="/images/fourth/image1.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>
