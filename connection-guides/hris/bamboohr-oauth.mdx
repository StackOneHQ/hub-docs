<Note>
  Administrator access is required in your account to manage API credentials and settings.
</Note>


## Getting your Company Domain

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in to BambooHR">
    Sign in to your BambooHR instance and note your company domain, which is the URL's prefix. For example, if your URL is `https://stackonetest.bamboohr.com`, then your Company Domain is **stackonetest**.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Company Domain" src="/images/bamboohr/image1.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>


## Connecting to StackOne

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter Company Domain">
    Upon reaching the Link Account page, enter the Company Domain and proceed by clicking the `Connect` button.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Enter Company Domain" src="/images/bamboohr/bamboohr_oauth_connector.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Login Credentials">
    You will be prompted to enter your login credentials.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="login credentials" src="/images/bamboohr/Login_screen.png" />
    </Frame>

    Click the `Yes, Trust this Browser` button.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Trust this Browser" src="/images/bamboohr/browser_trust.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>


## Linking your Account

Enter the required fields from the previous steps in your connection form.

- Employees
- Employment
- Groups
- Time Off
- Benefits
