If you've been directed to StackOne to integrate with Charlie HR, the following steps will help you understand the process and any necessary actions to configure successful integration.

## Getting your Client ID and Client Secret

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to Charlie HR">
    Log in to your Charlie HR dashboard, click on the Integrations tab.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Charlie HR Dashboard" src="/images/charliehr/image1.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Locate API Keys">
    Inside the new page, locate the API Keys option in the top navigation bar.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="API Setup" src="/images/charliehr/image2.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Copy Client ID and Client Key">
    Copy the keys to a safe place. 
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="API Keys" src="/images/charliehr/image3.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Connecting with StackOne

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter your Credentials">
    Enter your Client ID and Client Secret and proceed by clicking the `Connect` button.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Connect Button" src="/images/charliehr/image4.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>
