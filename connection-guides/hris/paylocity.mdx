If you've been directed to StackOne to integrate with Paylocity, the following steps will help you understand the process and any necessary actions to configure a successful integration.

## Getting the Credentials

<Steps>
    Ensure you have `Company ID` and OAuth credentials for `Weblink` and `NextGen` API's to connect with Paylocity.<br/>
    You must request these credentials from your Paylocity support contact. A partnership is not required; this integration will remain private only for your account.<br/>
    You can find more information on the <a href="https://developer.paylocity.com/integrations/v2025-08-12/docs/getting-started" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Paylocity's API page</a>.
</Steps>

## Connecting with StackOne

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter the Details">
    Upon reaching the Link Account page, enter the `Nextgen Client ID`, `Nextgen Client Secret`, `Weblink Client ID`, `Weblink Client Secret` and `Company ID`.<br/>
    Then proceed by clicking the **Connect** button.

  </Step>
</Steps>
