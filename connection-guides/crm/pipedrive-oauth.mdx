<Note>
  Administrator access is required in your Pipedrive account to create OAuth applications.
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Configure Company Domain">
    Click your **Profile icon** in the top-right corner and select **Company settings**.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/pipedrive-oauth/image1.png" style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Company Settings" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Company Details">
    Enter your **Company name** and **Company domain**. Copy and securely store your Company domain, then click **Save**.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/pipedrive-oauth/image2.png" style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Company Details" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Access Developer Hub">
    Click your **Profile icon** again and select **Developer Hub**.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/pipedrive-oauth/image3.png" style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Developer Hub" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create OAuth Application">
    Click **Create an App** to create a Public application.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/pipedrive-oauth/image4.png" style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Create Application" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure Application">
    Enter your **App name** and set the **Callback URL** to:
    `https://api.stackone.com/connect/oauth2/pipedrive/callback`

    Click **Save**.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/pipedrive-oauth/image5.png" style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="App Configuration" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select OAuth Scopes">
    In the **OAuth & access scopes** page, select the required permissions:
    - **Deals** - Full access
    - **Contacts** - Full access  
    - **Leads** - Full access

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/pipedrive-oauth/image6.png" style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="OAuth Scopes" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save OAuth Credentials">
    Copy your **Client ID** and **Client Secret** and store them securely. Click **Save**.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/pipedrive-oauth/image7.png" style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="OAuth Credentials" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Application Scopes

| Scope | Purpose | When to Use |
|--------|----------|-------------|
| Deals - Full access | Read and write deal information | Always required for CRM integrations |
| Contacts - Full access | Manage contact records | Required for contact synchronization |
| Leads - Full access | Handle lead management | Required for lead processing |

## Linking your Pipedrive Account

Enter these fields in your connection form:
- **Client ID** (from Step 7)
- **Client Secret** (from Step 7)
- **Company Domain** (from Step 2)
