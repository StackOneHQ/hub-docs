</Warning>

If you've been directed to StackOne to integrate with HubSpot, the following steps will help you understand the process and any necessary actions to configure successful integration.

## Starting the Connection Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Accounts Page">
    To get started, go to the Accounts page and follow the steps to connect your HubSpot account.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Accounts Page" src="/images/hubspot/image1.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Connecting to HubSpot

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Connect">
    Click the `Connect` button to open a window displaying the login form for HubSpot accounts.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Connect Button" src="/images/hubspot/image2.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Credentials">
    Enter your HubSpot credentials and click on `Log in`. This will show all your HubSpot accounts. Choose and click on the `Choose Account` button to connect.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Login Form" src="/images/hubspot/image3.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Account">
    Select the account you wish to connect with StackOne, then click on the `Choose Account` button.
  </Step>
</Steps>
