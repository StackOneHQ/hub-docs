  Ensure you have Admin privileges for your Attio account.
</Warning>

If you've been directed to StackOne to integrate with Attio, the following steps will help you understand the process and any necessary actions to configure successful integration.

## Generating an API key

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in to Attio">
    Sign in to the Attio Dashboard and click on your account name.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Attio Dashboard" src="/images/attio/image1.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Go to Account settings">
    Click on the Account settings option.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Account settings" src="/images/attio/image2.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open Developers settings">
    Inside the Settings page, click on the Developers option.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Developers settings" src="/images/attio/image3.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create a new integration">
    Click on Create a new integration button.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Create integration" src="/images/attio/image4.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add integration details">
    Add your integration name and, optionally, description/avatar. Afterward, click Create.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Integration details" src="/images/attio/image5.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Access tokens">
    Once you have successfully created your integration, click on Access tokens option.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Access tokens" src="/images/attio/image6.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Generate or copy API token">
    You can either generate a new access token (API Key) or copy the existing access token (API Key) for immediate use.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="API token" src="/images/attio/image7.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure integration scopes">
    Configure the integration scopes by clicking on the Scopes menu and setting the appropriate scope for your work â€“ `Read-write`.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Scopes" src="/images/attio/image8.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Connecting with StackOne

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter the Details">
    Upon reaching the Link Account page, enter the gathered details and proceed by clicking the `Connect` button.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Link Account" src="/images/attio/image9.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>
