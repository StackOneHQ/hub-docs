  This guidance assumes you have Admin privileges for your GO1 account.
</Warning>

## Linking to StackOne
  <Steps>
    <Step title="Link Go1 Account">
      In the StackOne Dashboard navigate to the `Accounts` page.

      Click `Link Account` and select Go1.

      Click `Connect`.
      <Frame>
        <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Link Account" src="/images/go1/image8.png" />
      </Frame>

      Log into your GO1 account with your username and password.

      Click `Authorize`

      <Frame>
        <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Link Account" src="/images/go1/image9.png" />
      </Frame>
    </Step>

  </Steps>
