<Warning>
  This guidance assumes you have Admin privileges for your GO1 account.
</Warning>

## Create Integrations App

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log into GO1">
    Log into your GO1 account.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Navigate to Integrations">
    Click on your profile tab in the top right corner.

    Select `Integrations` from the dropdown menu.

    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Dropdown Menu" src="/images/go1/image1.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Navigate to Developers Tab">
    From the `Integrations` menu on the left of the screen select `Developers`.

    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Dropdown Menu" src="/images/go1/image2.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create a New App">
    Click `Create App` in the top right.

    Enter the name of the app (e.g. Production, Development)

    Enter the callback URL (e.g. Production: https://api.stackone.com/connect/oauth2/go1/callback)

    `Production: https://api.stackone.com/connect/oauth2/go1/callback`
    `Development: https://api.stackone-dev.com/connect/oauth2/go1/callback`

    Click `Create Application`

    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Dropdown Menu" src="/images/go1/image3.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Note the Client ID and Secret">
    Record the `Client ID` and `Client Secret` for use in the next steps.

    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Dropdown Menu" src="/images/go1/image4.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

</Steps>


## Linking to StackOne

You can use the Client ID and Client Secret to link your GO1 account to StackOne.
  <Steps>
    <Step title="Enable Go1 Integration">
      In the StackOne Dashboard navigate to the `Integrations` page.

      Navigate to the `LMS` tab and click the `+` button next to GO1.

      <Frame>
        <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Enable Integration" src="/images/go1/image5.png" />
      </Frame>

      In the modal enter the `Client ID` and `Client Secret` from the previous step.

      <Frame>
        <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Enter Credentials" src="/images/go1/image6.png" />
      </Frame>
    </Step>

    <Step title="Link Go1 Account">
      In the StackOne Dashboard navigate to the `Accounts` page.

      Click `Link Account` and select Go1.

      <Frame>
        <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Link Account" src="/images/go1/image7.png" />
      </Frame>

      Click `Connect`.
    </Step>

  </Steps>
