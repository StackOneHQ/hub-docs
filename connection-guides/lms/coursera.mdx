## Get your Coursera API Credentials

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in to the Coursera Developer Portal">
    <ol>
      <li>Navigate to the <a href="https://dev.coursera.com" target="_blank">Coursera Developer Portal</a></li>
      <li>Click the <b>Sign In</b> button in the upper-right corner</li> 
      <li>Log in to your account at <a href="https://www.coursera.org" target="_blank">www.coursera.org</a> and click <b>Login With SAML</b></li>
      <li>Accept the terms and conditions if prompted</li>
    </ol>
    
    <Note>
      If the top-right still shows "Sign in" after logging in via SAML, click the Sign In button again.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Register an App">
    <ol>
      <li>Click your email address in the upper-right corner and choose `Apps` from the dropdown</li>
      <Frame><img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Apps dropdown" src="/images/coursera/image1.png" /></Frame>
      <li>Click `+ New App` in the top-right corner of the `Apps` page</li>
      <Frame><img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="+ New App" src="/images/coursera/image2.png" /></Frame>
      <li>Add a name and description for the new app and choose one or more APIs in the APIs section and click <b>Enable</b> to activate them</li>
      <Frame><img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Save App" src="/images/coursera/image3.png" /></Frame>
      <li>Click `Save` button to create an app.</li>
      <li>To view an API key or secret, click the `Show Secret` button in the Credentials section</li>
      <Frame><img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Show Credentials" src="/images/coursera/image4.png" /></Frame>
    </ol>
    
    <Note>
      Please use your organization's admin account to register apps to ensure you don't encounter any access issues.
    </Note>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Linking your Coursera Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter Credentials">
    In the hub, go to the Coursera tile.
    <ul>
      <li>Enter your `Client ID` (app key), `Client Secret` (app secret), and `Organization ID` from the Coursera Developer Portal</li>
    </ul>
    Click the <b>Connect</b> button to proceed.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Link Coursera Account" src="/images/coursera/image6.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Finding Your Organization ID

If you're looking for your organization ID to use in APIs, you can find it at the top of your organization's admin settings page in Coursera.
