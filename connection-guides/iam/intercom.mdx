<Warning>
  Ensure you have Admin privileges within your Intercom account.
</Warning>

If you've been directed to StackOne to integrate with Intercom, the following steps will help you understand the process and any necessary actions to configure successful integration.

This guidance assumes that you are an Intercom Admin.

## Get Your Intercom Access Token

This connection will be authenticated on behalf of a Private Intercom App you will create in your Intercom Developer Hub.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to Developer Hub">
    Log in to your Intercom [Developer Hub](https://app.intercom.com) to view Your apps.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Developer Hub" src="/images/intercom/image1.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create a New App">
    Click the New app button.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="New App Button" src="/images/intercom/image2.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter App Details">
    Under New app, enter the new App name and ensure the correct Workspace is selected.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="App Details" src="/images/intercom/image3.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create App">
    Click the Create app button to proceed.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Create App" src="/images/intercom/image4.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Copy Access Token">
    Your new App's Access token will be listed under Authentication. Copy your Access token and store it safely.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Access Token" src="/images/intercom/image5.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Connecting with StackOne

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter Access Token">
    Upon reaching the Link Account page, enter your Access Token from the previous step.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Link Account" src="/images/intercom/image6.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect">
    Proceed by clicking the Connect button.
  </Step>
</Steps>
