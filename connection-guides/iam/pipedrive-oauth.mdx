If you've been directed to StackOne to integrate with Pipedrive, the following steps will help you understand the process and any necessary actions to configure successful integration.

## Get Your Company Domain

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Company Settings">
    To access the Company settings, click on the Profile icon located in the top-right corner, and click Company settings in the menu that appears.

    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Company Settings" src="/images/pipedrive-oauth/image1.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Company Details">
    Under Company Settings, enter your Company name and Company domain. Copy and store your Company domain, then click the Save button.

    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Company Details" src="/images/pipedrive-oauth/image2.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Get Your Client ID and Client Secret

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Developer Hub">
    To access the Developer Hub, click on the Profile icon located in the top-right corner, and click Developer Hub in the menu that appears.

    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Developer Hub" src="/images/pipedrive-oauth/image3.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create an App">
    Click on the Create an App button to create a Public app.

    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Create an App" src="/images/pipedrive-oauth/image4.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter App Details">
    Enter your App name and the Callback URL written below, and click on the Save button.

    Callback URL: `https://api.stackone-dev.com/connect/oauth2/pipedrive/callback`

    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="App Details" src="/images/pipedrive-oauth/image5.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Access Scopes">
    You'll be directed to the OAuth & access scopes page. Select the following Access scopes for your app:
    - Deals - Full access
    - Contacts - Full access
    - Leads - Full access

    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Access Scopes" src="/images/pipedrive-oauth/image6.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Copy Credentials">
    Scroll down to Client ID. Copy the displayed Client ID and Client Secret and store them safely, then click the Save button.

    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Client ID and Secret" src="/images/pipedrive-oauth/image7.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Connecting with StackOne

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter Credentials">
    Upon reaching the Link Account page, enter the credentials from the previous steps:
    - Client ID
    - Client Secret
    - Company Domain

    Proceed by clicking the Connect button.

    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Link Account" src="/images/pipedrive-oauth/image8.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>


## Linking your Account

Enter the required fields from the previous steps in your connection form.
