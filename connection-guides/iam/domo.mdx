<Warning>
  Ensure you have the Create API OAuth Clients Grant assigned to your Domo Role. This is available in the default Domo Roles: Admin, Privileged, Editor, Participant, and Social.
</Warning>

If you've been directed to StackOne to integrate with Domo, the following steps will help you understand the process and any necessary actions to configure successful integration.

## Get Your Domo Client ID and Client Secret

<Steps>
  <Step title="Login to Domo Developer Portal">
    Log in to your [Domo Developer Portal](https://developer.domo.com) by entering your Domain.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Domo Developer Portal" src="/images/domo/image1.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Navigate to Manage Clients">
    Go to the [Manage Clients](https://developer.domo.com/manage-clients) page by clicking Developer > Manage Client IDs on the top.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Manage Clients" src="/images/domo/image2.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create a New Client">
    Any existing clients will be listed here. Click Create a client at the bottom to create a new set of credentials.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Client Details">
    Under Create new client, enter the required information:
    <ul>
      <li>Name</li>
      <li>Application Scope: User</li>
    </ul>
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Create Client" src="/images/domo/image3.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Generate Credentials">
    Click the Create button to continue. Your new Client credentials will now be displayed on the Manage Clients page. Copy your Client ID and Client Secret and store them safely.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Connecting with StackOne

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter Credentials">
    Upon reaching the Link Account page, enter your Client ID and Client Secret from the previous step.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Link Account" src="/images/domo/image4.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect">
    Proceed by clicking the `Connect` button.
  </Step>
</Steps>


## Linking your Account

Enter the required fields from the previous steps in your connection form.
