If you've been directed to StackOne to integrate with Webflow, the following steps will help you understand the process and any necessary actions to configure a successful integration.

## Log in to Webflow

    <Step title="Log in to Webflow">
        Log in to your [Webflow app](https://webflow.com/login)

        <Frame>
            <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Log in" src="/images/webflow/image1.png" />
        </Frame>
    </Step>

## Get your Webflow Site ID

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Sites">
    Select `All sites` from the left menu and select `Settings` from the three dots in front of your Site.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="All sites" src="/images/webflow/image2.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Copy Site ID">
    Under `General`, scroll down to `Overview`. Copy and store the **Site ID** safely.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Site Settings" src="/images/webflow/image3.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Create a New OAuth Application

This connection will authenticate on behalf of a registered Application in Webflow.
<Steps>
    
    <Step title="Navigate to App development">
        Select `Settings > Apps & Integrations` from the left menu and scroll down to `App development`.

        <Frame>
            <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Navigate to App development" src="/images/webflow/image4.png" />
        </Frame>
    </Step>

    
    <Step title="Create New Application">
        Click on <Icon icon="plus" iconType="regular" /> `Create an App` button.

        <Frame>
            <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Create New Application" src="/images/webflow/image5.png" />
        </Frame>
    </Step>

    
    <Step title="Enter Application Details">
        Enter an App name, description and homepage URL. Then, click on the `Continue` button.

        <Frame>
            <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Enter Application Details" src="/images/webflow/image6.png" />
        </Frame>
    </Step>

    <Step title="Enter Redirect URI">
        Enable the `Data client (REST API)` toggle button and Enter the Redirect URI: `https://api.stackone.com/connect/oauth2/webflow_iam/callback`

        <Frame>
            <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Enter Redirect URI" src="/images/webflow/image7.png" />
        </Frame>
    </Step>

    
    <Step title="Select Application Scopes">
        Scroll down and select `Read-only` access for `User Account` and then click on the `Create App` button.

        <Frame>
            <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Select Application Scopes" src="/images/webflow/image8.png" />
        </Frame>
    </Step>

    
    <Step title="Copy Application Credentials">
        Upon successfully creating your app, copy the new Client ID and Client Secret that are displayed, and store them safely.

        <Frame>
            <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Copy Application Credentials" src="/images/webflow/image9.png" />
        </Frame>
    </Step>

</Steps>


## Connecting with StackOne

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter Credentials">
    Upon reaching the Link Account page, enter the credentials from the previous steps:
    - Client ID
    - Client Secret
    - Site ID
    - Application Scopes

    Proceed by clicking the Connect button.

    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Enter Credentials" src="/images/webflow/image10.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>
