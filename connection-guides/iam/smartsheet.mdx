If you've been directed to StackOne to integrate with Smartsheet, the following steps will help you understand the process and any necessary actions to configure a successful integration.

## Log in to Smartsheet

Log in to your Smartsheet account at https://app.smartsheet.com 

<Frame>
    <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Log in" src="/images/smartsheet/image1.png" />
</Frame>


## Create a New Access Token

<Steps>
    
    <Step title="Navigate to Access Tokens">
        Click your profile icon in the bottom left corner, then click **Apps & Integrations...**

        <Frame>
            <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Navigate to Access Tokens" src="/images/smartsheet/image6.png" />
        </Frame>
    </Step>

    
    <Step title="Create New Access Token">
        Under *Personal Settings*, click **API Access**, then click the **Generate new access token** button.

        <Frame>
            <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Create New Access Token" src="/images/smartsheet/image7.png" />
        </Frame>
    </Step>

    
    <Step title="Enter Access Token Details">
        Enter a **Name** for the new token, then click the **OK** button.

        <Frame>
            <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Enter Access Token Details" src="/images/smartsheet/image8.png" />
        </Frame>
    </Step>

    
    <Step title="Copy your Access Token">
        Copy your displayed **Access Token** to be used in the next step.

        <Frame>
            <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Copy your Access Token" src="/images/smartsheet/image9.png" />
        </Frame>
    </Step>

</Steps>


## Connecting with StackOne

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter Credentials">
    Upon reaching the Link Account page, enter the **Access Token** from the previous step.

    Proceed by clicking the Connect button.

    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Enter Credentials" src="/images/smartsheet/image1.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>


## Linking your Account

Enter the required fields from the previous steps in your connection form.
