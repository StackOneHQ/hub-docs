If you've been directed to StackOne to integrate with SmartRecruiters, the following steps will help you understand the process and any necessary actions to configure successful integration.

## Generating Client ID and Clients Secret

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Settings">
    On the Home page, click on your Account avatar and select Settings.

    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Account Avatar" src="/images/smartrecruiters/image1.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Go to Apps & Integrations">
    On the Settings page, click on Apps & Integrations under the Administration section.

    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Apps & Integrations" src="/images/smartrecruiters/image2.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open Credentials">
    On the Apps & Integrations page, click on CREDENTIALS.

    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Credentials" src="/images/smartrecruiters/image3.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create New Credentials">
    On the CREDENTIALS page, click on the NEW CREDENTIALS button.

    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="New Credentials" src="/images/smartrecruiters/image4.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select OAuth Client ID">
    Under the NEW CREDENTIALS section, select OAuth client ID then click on the NEXT button.

    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="OAuth Client ID" src="/images/smartrecruiters/image5.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Generate Client ID and Secret">
    On the OAuth client ID page, fill in the required Fields and select Scope, then click on the GENERATE button at the bottom of the page.

    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Generate Credentials" src="/images/smartrecruiters/image6.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Copy Client ID and Secret">
    Copy your Client ID and Clients Secret by clicking Clipboard.

    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Copy Credentials" src="/images/smartrecruiters/image7.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Utilizing Client ID and Clients Secret

<Steps>

  </Step>
</Steps>
