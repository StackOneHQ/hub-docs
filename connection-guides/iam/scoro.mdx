If you've been directed to StackOne to integrate with Scoro, the following steps will help you understand the process and any necessary actions to configure successful integration.

This guidance assumes that you are an Administrator within Scoro.

## Get User Credentials

You will need to provide a Username and Password and Company Account Id . It is recommended that you set up a new account for integration purposes.
<Steps>
 <Step title="Log in to Scoro">
    Log in to your Scoro Account.  `https://{Your Company Account ID}.scoro.com/login`
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Login" src="/images/scoro/image1.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Go to manage users">
      Click on the dropdown button from top right corner and then click on the `Manage users` tab.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Manage users" src="/images/scoro/image2.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

    <Step title="Create a New User">
    Under the Users and Groups, click the `Users` tab and then click `Add User` button to create a new user.

    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Add New User" src="/images/scoro/image3.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

    <Step title="Fill Out Required Fields">
    Fill out the required fields, check the `Has administrator rights` and \
    `Send an invitation e-mail` checkboxes, and select **Full access** in the `Permission set` dropdown. This will ensure the account has the correct permissions. Afterward, click `Create user`

    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Fill form" src="/images/scoro/image4.png" />

## Get Your Company Account ID

Your **Company Account ID** can be found in the URL when you are logged in to Scoro. 

Given the URL: `https://my-company.scoro.com/settings`, your **Company Account ID** is `my-company`.


## Connecting with StackOne

<Steps> 
  <Step title="Enter Credentials">
    Upon reaching the Link Account page, enter your credentials from the previous steps:

    - Company Account ID
    - Username
    - Password

    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Integration connections" src="/images/scoro/image8.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect">
    Click the Connect button to proceed.
  </Step>
</Steps>
 

## Linking your Account

Enter the required fields from the previous steps in your connection form.
