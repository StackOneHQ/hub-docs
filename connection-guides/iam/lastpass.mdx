  Ensure you have Admin privileges for your LastPass Enterprise account.
</Warning>

If you've been directed to StackOne to integrate with LastPass, the following steps will help you understand the process and any necessary actions to configure successful integration.

## Get your LastPass CID (Account Number)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to LastPass Admin Console">
    Log in to your LastPass Admin Console at [https://admin.lastpass.com](https://admin.lastpass.com)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Access Security Dashboard">
    On the left, click Security dashboard.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Security Dashboard" src="/images/lastpass/image1.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Locate CID">
    In the Security Dashboard, your CID is located next to "Account number". Store this safely.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="CID Location" src="/images/lastpass/image2.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Get your LastPass Provisioning Hash

This token is required for accessing the LastPass Enterprise API.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Enterprise API">
    Within the Admin Console, click Advanced in the top navigation.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Navigate to Enterprise API">
    On the left, click Enterprise API.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Enterprise API" src="/images/lastpass/image3.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create Provisioning Hash">
    Click the Create your provisioning hash button. If this hash has already been generated but is lost, click the Reset your provisioning hash button.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Create Provisioning Hash" src="/images/lastpass/image4.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Handle Hash Reset Warning">
    If resetting the hash, a warning message will appear. Click Ok to proceed.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Reset Hash Warning" src="/images/lastpass/image5.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Copy Provisioning Hash">
    Your new provisioning hash will be displayed only once. Copy and store it safely.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Provisioning Hash" src="/images/lastpass/image6.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Connecting to StackOne

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter Credentials">
    Upon reaching the Link Account page, enter your CID (Account Number) and Provisioning Hash from the previous steps.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Link Account" src="/images/lastpass/image7.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect">
    Proceed by clicking the Connect button.
  </Step>
</Steps>
