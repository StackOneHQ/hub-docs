If you've been directed to StackOne to integrate with Teamviewer, the following steps will help you understand the process and any necessary actions to configure a successful integration.

## Log in to Teamviewer

Log in to your Teamviewer account at https://account.teamviewer.com

<Frame>
    <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Log in" src="/images/teamviewer/image1.png" />
</Frame>

## Create a New Script Token

<Steps>
    
    <Step title="Navigate to Script Tokens">
        Go to your Teamviewer Management Console at https://login.teamviewer.com/nav/managecompany/apps

        Click **Company Administration** on the left, then click **Apps**.

        <Frame>
            <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Navigate to Script Tokens" src="/images/teamviewer/image2.png" />
        </Frame>
    </Step>

    
    <Step title="Create New Script Token">
        Under *Apps and Scripts accessing your company*, click the **Create script token** button 

        <Frame>
            <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Create New Script Token" src="/images/teamviewer/image3.png" />
        </Frame>
    </Step>

    
    <Step title="Select Script Token Permissions">
        In the winow that appears on the right, enter a **Name** for this new token.

        Select the following two permissions required for this integration:
        - User management: **view users**
        - Group management: **read groups**

        Click the **Create** button to proceed.

        <Frame>
            <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Select Script Token Permissions" src="/images/teamviewer/image4.png" />
        </Frame>
    </Step>

    
    <Step title="Copy your Script Token">
        Next to your new listed Script Token, click the *Copy* button and store it securely to be used in the next step.

        <Frame>
            <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Copy your Script Token" src="/images/teamviewer/image5.png" />
        </Frame>
    </Step>

</Steps>


## Connecting with StackOne
Upon reaching the Link Account page, enter your **Script Token** created in the previous step.

Proceed by clicking the Connect button.


## Linking your Account

Enter the required fields from the previous steps in your connection form.
