  Ensure you have Owner privileges within your Rollbar account.
</Warning>

If you've been directed to StackOne to integrate with Rollbar, the following steps will help you understand the process and any necessary actions to configure successful integration.

## Get Your Rollbar Access Token

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to Rollbar">
    Log in to your [Rollbar Account](https://rollbar.com/login/). 
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Log in to Rollbar" src="/images/rollbar/image1.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
    <Step title="Navigate to Account Access Tokens">
    Click the <Icon icon="gear" iconType="regular" /> `Settings` button on the left, then click `Setup > Account Access Tokens`.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Account Access Tokens" src="/images/rollbar/image2.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Account Access Token">
    Click the `Add new access token` button. The Account Access Token is now listed here.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Add Account Access Token" src="/images/rollbar/image3.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add read scope">
    Click the pencil <Icon icon="pencil" iconType="solid" /> icon of your Token, then update the name if required and check the `read` scope box. Click the check <Icon icon="check" iconType="solid" /> icon once done.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Add scope" src="/images/rollbar/image4.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Copy Access Token">
    Copy and store the Account Access Token safely.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Copy Access Token" src="/images/rollbar/image5.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Connecting to StackOne

Upon reaching the Link Account page, enter the **Account Access Token** from the previous step. Proceed by clicking the Connect button.
<Frame>
  <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Link Account" src="/images/rollbar/image6.png" />
</Frame>
