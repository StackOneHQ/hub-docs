If you've been directed to StackOne to integrate with Spotdraft, the following steps will help you understand the process and any necessary actions to configure successful integration.

## Get user credentials

You will need to provide a user email and password. It is recommended that you set up a new account for integration purposes.

<Steps>
<Step title="Log in to Spotdraft">
    Log in to your [Spotdraft Account](https://app.spotdraft.com/auth/login).
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Login" src="/images/spotdraft/image1.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to settings">
    Hover on the `Settings` <Icon icon="gear" iconType="regular" /> button from the left menu and click on the `Access Control` tab.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Settings" src="/images/spotdraft/image2.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create a New User">
    Under the `Team Members` tab, click the `âž• Add New Member` to create a new employee.

    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="New User" src="/images/spotdraft/image3.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Fill Out Required Fields">
    Fill out the required fields and set the Team to `Admin`. This will ensure the account has the correct permissions, and then click `Send Invite` button.

    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Set Role" src="/images/spotdraft/image4.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create Account">
    The email address you entered will receive an invitation. Click `JOIN NOW`, you may have to log out of your current account to create the password.

    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Create Account" src="/images/spotdraft/image5.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Get Account Email and Password">
    You should now have the account email address and password to proceed with the Hub.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Connecting to StackOne

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter User Details">
    Upon reaching the Link Account page, enter the Username and Password from the previous step. Proceed by clicking the Connect button.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Link Account" src="/images/spotdraft/image6.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>


## Linking your Account

Enter the required fields from the previous steps in your connection form.
