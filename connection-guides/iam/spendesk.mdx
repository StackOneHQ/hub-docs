If you've been directed to StackOne to integrate with Spendesk, the following steps will help you understand the process and any necessary actions to configure successful integration.

## Get OAuth Credentials

<Steps>
 <Step title="Get Partner Credentials">
    To get credentials you need to contact with Spendesk partnership team. Spendesk will generate and share the credentials.\
    [Contact the Spendesk Partnership team]( https://www.spendesk.com/partners/).
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Login" src="/images/spendesk/image.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Connecting to StackOne

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter Authentication Details">
    Upon reaching the Link Account page, enter the following authentication details from the previous steps:
    <ul>
      <li>Client ID</li>
      <li>Client Secret</li>
      <li>Scopes  `user:read`</li>
    </ul>
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Link Account" src="/images/spendesk/image1.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect">
    Proceed by clicking the Connect button.
  </Step>
</Steps>


## Linking your Account

Enter the required fields from the previous steps in your connection form.
