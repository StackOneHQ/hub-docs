If you've been directed to StackOne to integrate with Kameleoon, the following steps will help you understand the process and any necessary actions to configure a successful integration.

## Log in to Kameleoon

Log in to your Kameleoon account.

<Frame>
    <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Log in to Kameleoon" src="/images/kameleoon/image1.png" />
</Frame>


## Obtain your Client ID and Client Secret

This integration will authenticate on behalf of your Kameleoon user API credentials.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to your Profile">
      Click your profile icon in the top-right corner, then click **My Profile**.
  
      <Frame>
          <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Navigate to your Profile" src="/images/kameleoon/image2.png" />
      </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Generate or view your client credentials">
      Under your profile name, click **Generate new API credentials**, or **See my API credentials**.
  
      If you already have a Client ID and Client Secret, you can use these for this connection.
      <Frame>
          <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Create a new Client ID" src="/images/kameleoon/image3.png" />
      </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Copy your Client ID and Client Secret">
      If you are generating new credentials, copy your new **Client ID** and **Client Secret** and store them safely to be used in the next step.
      <Frame>
          <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Copy your Client ID and Client Secret" src="/images/kameleoon/image4.png" />
      </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Connecting with StackOne

    Upon reaching the Link Account page, enter the credential(s) from the previous steps:
    - **Client ID**
    - **Client Secret**

    Proceed by clicking the Connect button.

    <Frame>
        <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Enter Credentials" src="/images/kameleoon/image5.png" />
    </Frame>


## Linking your Account

Enter the required fields from the previous steps in your connection form.
