If you've been directed to StackOne to integrate with Databricks, the following steps will help you understand the process and any necessary actions to configure a successful integration.

## Log in to Databricks

Log in to your Databricks instance at https://accounts.cloud.databricks.com

<Frame>
    <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Log in" src="/images/databricks/image1.png" />
</Frame>

## Obtain your Account ID

Click the down arrow next to your username in the top-right corner. In the drop-down menu, copy your displayed **Account ID** to be used in a later step.

<Frame>
    <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Obtain your Account ID" src="/images/databricks/image2.png" />
</Frame>

## Obtain your SCIM Token

<Steps>
    
    <Step title="Navigate to User Provisioning">
        Click <Icon icon="gear" iconType="regular" /> `Settings` in the left navigation, then **User Provisioning**.

        <Frame>
            <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Navigate to User Provisioning" src="/images/databricks/image3.png" />
        </Frame>
    </Step>
    
    <Step title="Copy your SCIM Token">
        If User Provisioning is enabled for your account, your **SCIM Token** will be displayed under *User Provisioning*. Copy and store it securely to be used in the next step.
    </Step>

</Steps>


## Connecting with StackOne

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter Credentials">
    Upon reaching the Link Account page, enter the credentials from the previous steps:
    - **Account ID**
    - **SCIM Token**

    Proceed by clicking the Connect button.
  </Step>
</Steps>


## Linking your Account

Enter the required fields from the previous steps in your connection form.
