If you've been directed to StackOne to integrate with Airship, the following steps will help you understand the process and any necessary actions to configure successful integration.

## Find your Airship region (EU or US)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Login to Airship">
    Login to your Airship tenant.

    Check your browser's URL bar. The URL will indicate which region you are on:
    `https://go.urbanairship.com` - US
    `https://go.airship.eu/` - EU
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Airship Region" src="/images/airship/image1.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Retrieve your App Key

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Project">
    Select the project you want to connect.

    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Select Project" src="/images/airship/image2.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Settings">
    Select Settings.

    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Select Settings" src="/images/airship/image3.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Copy App Key">
    Within Project Details on the right side, copy the App Key.

    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="App Key" src="/images/airship/image4.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Retrieve your Master Secret

<Steps>
  <Step title="Reveal Master Secret">
    Within the Project Details panel, select "Click to reveal" next to Master Secret.

    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Reveal Master Secret" src="/images/airship/image5.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Copy Master Secret">
    Copy the contents of Master Secret.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Input the collected inputs into the StackOne hub

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter the Details">
    On the link account page, enter the details you have collected and click the `Connect` button.

    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Link Account" src="/images/airship/image6.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>


## Linking your Account

Enter the required fields from the previous steps in your connection form.
