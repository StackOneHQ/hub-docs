  Ensure that your Sharepoint account has Admin or Owner privileges.
</Warning>

If you've been directed to StackOne to integrate with Sharepoint, the following steps will help you understand the process and any necessary actions to configure a successful integration.

## Log in to Sharepoint

<Steps>
    <Step title="Connect to Sharepoint">
        Click the Connect button to start the login process. You will be redirected to Sharepoint.
    
        <Frame>
            <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Connect to GoogleDrive" src="/images/sharepoint/image-1.png" />
        </Frame>
    </Step>
    <Step title="Grant OAuth Application Consent">
      A window will appear and may prompt you to log in to your Sharepoint account.

      After logging in, you will be displayed the OAuth consent screen.

      Click the **Allow** button to grant the application access to your Sharepoint account.
    </Step>
</Steps>
