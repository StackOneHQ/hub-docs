  Ensure that your Notion account has Workspace Owner privileges.
</Warning>

If you've been directed to StackOne to integrate with Notion, the following steps will help you understand the process and any necessary actions to configure a successful integration.

## Log in to Notion

<Steps>
    <Step title="Connect to Notion">
        Click the Connect button to start the login process.
    
        <Frame>
            <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Connect to Notion" src="/images/notion-oauth/image1.png" />
        </Frame>
    </Step>
    <Step title="Enter Credentials">
        Enter your Notion credentials or use one of the sign-in options available that are connected to your Notion account.
    
        <Frame>
            <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Enter Credentials" src="/images/notion/image2.png" />
        </Frame>
    </Step>
</Steps>

## Select the Pages to access

<Steps>
    <Step title="Review Permissions">
        A window will appear with list of permissions to review. Click on `Select pages` button to continue 
    
        <Frame>
            <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Review Permissions" src="/images/notion-oauth/image2.png" />
        </Frame>
    </Step>
    <Step title="Select pages to access">
        Select all the pages, which you want to access and click on `Allow access` button.
    
        <Frame>
            <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Select pages to access" src="/images/notion-oauth/image3.png" />
        </Frame>
    </Step>
</Steps>



## Linking your Account

Enter the required fields from the previous steps in your connection form.
