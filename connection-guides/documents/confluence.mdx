If you've been directed to StackOne to integrate with Confluence, the following steps will help you understand the process and any necessary actions to configure a successful integration.

## Log in to Confluence

Log in to your Confluence account at https://id.atlassian.com/login.

## Create OAuth 2.0 Credentials for Service Accounts

<Note>
This integration uses OAuth 2.0 service accounts which provide secure, granular access to Confluence. The following OAuth scopes are required:
- `search:confluence` - Search across Confluence content
- `read:confluence-space.summary` - Read space summaries
- `read:space:confluence` - Access spaces (/api/v2/spaces)
- `read:page:confluence` - Access pages (/api/v2/pages)
- `read:content:confluence` - Search and export content (/rest/api/content/search)
- `write:page:confluence` - Create and update pages
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Developer Console">
      Go to the [Atlassian Developer Console](https://developer.atlassian.com/console/myapps/) and log in with your Atlassian account.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create OAuth 2.0 Integration">
      Click **Create** and select **OAuth 2.0 integration** to create a new OAuth app.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure Your App">
      Enter a name for your OAuth integration and click **Create**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure Permissions">
      Navigate to the **Permissions** tab and add the following OAuth 2.0 scopes:
      - `search:confluence`
      - `read:confluence-space.summary`
      - `read:space:confluence`
      - `read:page:confluence`
      - `read:content:confluence`
      - `write:page:confluence`
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create Service Account Credentials">
      Navigate to the **Authorization** tab and create OAuth 2.0 credentials for a service account. Copy the **Client ID** and **Client Secret** - you'll need these for linking your account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Linking your Account

Upon reaching the Link Account page, select Confluence integration from the hub and enter the credentials from the previous steps:
  - **Client ID**
  - **Client Secret**
  - **Domain Name**

Proceed by clicking the Connect button to complete the OAuth authorization flow.
