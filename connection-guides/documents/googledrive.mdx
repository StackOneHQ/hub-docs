If you've been directed to StackOne to integrate with GoogleDrive, the following steps will help you understand the process and any necessary actions to configure a successful integration.

## Log in to GoogleDrive

<Steps>
    <Step title="Connect to GoogleDrive">
        Click the Connect button to start the login process. You will be redirected to Google Drive.
    
        <Frame>
            <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Connect to GoogleDrive" src="/images/googledrive/image1.png" />
        </Frame>
    </Step>
    <Step title="Grant OAuth Application Consent">
      A window will appear and may prompt you to log in to your Google Drive account.

      After logging in, you will be displayed the OAuth consent screen.

      Click the **Allow** button to grant the application access to your Google Drive account.

          <Frame>
        <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Grant OAuth Application Consent" src="/images/googledrive/image2.png" />
      </Frame>
    </Step>
</Steps>


## Linking your Account

Enter the required fields from the previous steps in your connection form.
