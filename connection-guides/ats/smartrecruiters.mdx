This guidance assumes you have Admin privileges for your SmartRecruiters account.

 

SmartRecruiters — Counterview Capital
 

Getting Started with SmartRecruiters API

 

Step 1: Generating Client ID and Clients Secret.
1.On the Home page, click on your Account avatar and select Settings.
​

![Step 1](/images/humaans/image1.png) 

 


 

2.On the Settings page, click on Apps & Integrations under Administrations section.

 
![Step 2](/images/humaans/image2.png) 


 

3. On the Apps & Integrations page, click on CREDENTIALS.

 

![Step 3](/images/humaans/image3.png) 

 

 

4. On the CREDENTIALS page, click on NEW CREDENTIALS button.

 
![Step 4](/images/humaans/image4.png) 


 

5. Under NEW CREDENTIALS section, select OAuth client ID then click on NEXT button.

 

![Step 5](/images/humaans/image5.png) 

 

6. On OAuth client ID page, fill the required Fields and select Scope, then click on GENERATE button at the bottom of the page.

 
![Step 6](/images/humaans/image6.png) 


 

7.Copy your Client ID and Clients Secret by clicking Clipboard.
​

![Step 7](/images/humaans/image7.png) 

 

 

Step 2: Utilising Client ID and Clients Secret.
Upon reaching the Link Account page, enter the gathered details and proceed by clicking the Connect button.

 
![Step 8](/images/humaans/image8.png)
