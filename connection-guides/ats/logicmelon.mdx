If you've been directed to StackOne to integrate with LogicMelon, the following steps will help you understand the process and any necessary actions to configure successful integration.

## Getting the API key

<Steps>
  Please contact LogicMelon support to get the API key on your registered mail.
</Steps>

## Connecting with StackOne

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter the Details">
    Upon reaching the Link Account page, enter the gathered details and proceed by clicking the `Connect` button.

    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Link Account" src="/images/logicmelon/image1.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>


## Linking your Account

Enter the required fields from the previous steps in your connection form.

<IntegrationFooter />
