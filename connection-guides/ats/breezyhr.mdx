If you've been directed to StackOne to integrate with Breezy HR, the following steps will help you understand the process and any necessary actions to configure a successful integration.

## Log in to Breezy HR

Log in to your Breezy HR account at https://app.breezy.hr/signin.

    <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Log in to Breezy HR" src="/images/breezyhr/image1.png" />

## Create a new Breezy HR System User

This integration will authenticate on behalf of a specified Breezy HR Company Administrator.

You can use an existing User, or follow the steps below to create a new User.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Users">
      Click the Settings icon <Icon icon="gear" iconType="solid" color="#333333" /> in the left navigation.
  
      <Frame>
          <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Navigate to Users" src="/images/breezyhr/image2.png" />
      </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a new User">
      Under Company Settings, click **Users**.

      Click **Invite Members** to create a new User.
  
      <Frame>
          <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Create a new User" src="/images/breezyhr/image3.png" />
      </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send Invitation">
      Enter the new User's Email Address. Then select **Administrator** in the dropdown box labeled "Add to this Role".

      Click the **Invite User(s)** button to complete.
  
      <Frame>
          <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Send Invitation" src="/images/breezyhr/image4.png" />
      </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Accept Invitation">
      An invitation from Breezy HR will be sent to the email address submitted above. Accept the invitation to complete the User creation process.

      This User's email and password will be required for this connection.
  </Step>
</Steps>
## Find your Company ID

Your Company ID can be found in the URL when you are logged into Breezy HR, located directly after `app.breezy.hr/app/`.

Given the URL https://app.breezy.hr/app/my-company/home, your Company ID is `my-company`.

## Connecting with StackOne

Upon reaching the Link Account page, enter the credential(s) from the previous steps:
  - **Email Address**
  - **Password**
  - **Company ID**

Proceed by clicking the Connect button.
