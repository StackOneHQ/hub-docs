If you've been directed to StackOne to integrate with UKG Pro, the following steps will help you understand the process and any necessary actions to configure successful integration.

## Getting your Host, Tenant Alias, Client ID and Client Secret

These are unique identifiers for your account. If you do not possess this information, please contact to <a href="https://www.ukg.com/support">UKG Pro support</a>.

## Connecting to StackOne

1. Enter your Host, Tenanat Alias, Client ID and Client Secret from Step 1.
2. Click the `Connect` button.
