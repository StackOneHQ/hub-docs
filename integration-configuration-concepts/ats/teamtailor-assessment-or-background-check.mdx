## Send the assessment/background check invitation to the candidate

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Candidate from the Job">
    Navigate to the <i>Job's</i> page and move one of the candidate to the specific stage where you want to trigger assessment/background check.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Maintain Assessment Status" src="/images/teamtailor-assessment/send_assess_1.png" />
    </Frame>

    Click on the candidate to open their application. Then, click on the three-dot more options in the lower bar and select `Add Integration`.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Maintain Assessment Status" src="/images/teamtailor-assessment/send_assess_2.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="To Select Integration & Send the Notification">
    Select the partner integration you want to use to send the assessment/background check to the candidate.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Edit/View Assessment/Background check Status ID" src="/images/teamtailor-assessment/send_assess_3.png" />
    </Frame>

    A pop-up will appear to select the assessment/background check test. Below is an example of an assessment test.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Edit/View Assessment/Background check Status ID" src="/images/teamtailor-assessment/send_assessment_test.png" />
    </Frame>

    Click the `Send webhook` button to dispatch the notification, and you will see a success response on the same page.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Edit/View Assessment/Background check Status ID" src="/images/teamtailor-assessment/send_assess_5.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## To check updated result of the assessment/background check invitation of the candidate

<Steps>
  <Step title="To see the updated results of assessment/background check">
    Click on the candidate's name to view their application. You will see the updated results along with the score from the previously sent webhook.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="View Assessment/Background check Updated Results" src="/images/teamtailor-assessment/updates_result_1.png" />
    </Frame>
    Whenever the candidate has completed the assessment and background check.

  </Step>
</Steps>
