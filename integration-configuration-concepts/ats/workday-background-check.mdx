## Submit a job application for the candidate in Workday

<Steps>
  <Step title="Apply for a job">
    Navigate to the <i>Find Candidates</i> page, select a candidate's profile, and click on `Job Application` under `Actions` to apply for the job.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Candidate Profile" src="/images/workday-background-check/workday_bc_candidate_profile_action_apply_to_job_17.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select the job requisition">
    From the dropdown menu, select the relevant job.

    <Accordion title="Finding Job Requisitions within your Supervisory Organisation">
      <Warning>
        These steps assume you have a Supervisory Organisation in your Workday account. 
        If not, please contact your administrator to create one. You will also need the necessary permissions to access the _Supervisory Organisation_ in Workday.
      </Warning>

      <Step title="Available job requisitions">
        Navigate to your <i>Supervisory Organisation</i> page and go to the `Staffing` tab.
        <Info>
          Ensure the Supervisory Organisation's Staffing Model is `Position Management`
        </Info>
        <Frame>
          <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="SO for JR" src="/images/workday-background-check/SO_for_JR.png" />
        </Frame>

        Under the `Positions with Open Job Requisition` section, you'll find a list of job requisitions that are configured to send notifications for the background check stage.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Creating a new job requisition">
        If no jobs are listed under `Positions with Open Job Requisition`, find `Create Position` under the `Staffing` tab in the three-dot menu.
        <Frame>
          <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Create Job Position" src="/images/workday-background-check/SO_1.png" />
        </Frame>

        Complete the required details to create a position. It will then appear in the `Positions without Job Requisition` section.
        <Frame>
          <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Created Job Position" src="/images/workday-background-check/SO_2.png" />
        </Frame>

        Navigate to `Create Job Requisition`, select your supervisory organisation and position, click `OK`, complete the Job Requisition form, and then `Submit` it.
        <Frame>
          <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Add in Position in Job Req" src="/images/workday-background-check/SO_3.png" />
        </Frame>

        The position will now appear in the `In Progress Requisition Actions` section.
        <Frame>
          <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Added in Position in Job Req" src="/images/workday-background-check/SO_4.png" />
        </Frame>

        Once the job requisition is approved, it will appear in the `Positions with Open Job Requisition` section and will be enabled for notifications.
        <Frame>
          <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Approved Job Req" src="/images/workday-background-check/SO_5.png" />
        </Frame>
      </Step>
    </Accordion>

    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Candidate Job Apply" src="/images/workday-background-check/workday_bc_candidate_profile_applied_to_job_18.png" />
    </Frame>

    Complete the required details and click the `OK` button to submit the job application.
  </Step>
</Steps>


## Move the candidate to the Background Check stage in Workday

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to the Job Requisition">
    Go to the <i>Job Requisitions</i> page by selecting the same Job Requisition for which the candidate has applied.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="View JR" src="/images/workday-background-check/workday_bc_view_JR_19.png" />
    </Frame>

    Navigate to the `Candidate` tab and select the `Review` tab, where the candidate will be displayed in the grid.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Candidate Job Apply" src="/images/workday-background-check/workday_bc_JR_20.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Move the candidate from Review to Background Check stage">
    Select the candidate in the grid and click the `Move Forward` button.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="View JR" src="/images/workday-background-check/workday_bc_jr_move_button_21.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select the Background Check stage">
    In the pop-up window, select the next stage. If the Background Check stage isn't immediately available, you may need to move through stages one at a time. For the Background Check stage, choose `Background Check` in both dropdown menus.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Candidate Job Apply" src="/images/workday-background-check/workday_bc_jr_moved_to_bc_stage_22.png" />
    </Frame>

    Click the `OK` button to move the candidate.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm the candidate has moved to the Background Check stage">
    Navigate to the `Background Check` tab to confirm the candidate is now in the Background Check stage.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="View JR" src="/images/workday-background-check/workday_bc_jr_bc_satge_success_23.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>


## Send a background check invitation to the candidate

<Steps>
  <Step title="Submit a background check as a recruiter">
    As the recruiter, you should see the `Background Check` button next to the candidate's name.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Sending Invite" src="/images/workday-background-check/workday_bc_jr_bc_stage_button_to_submit_24.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Send the background check invitation">
    Select the Background Check Package from the dropdown menu.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Send Invite" src="/images/workday-background-check/workday_bc_jr_bc_submission_25.png" />
    </Frame>
    Click the `Submit` button to trigger and send the webhook notification from Workday. 
  </Step>
</Steps>


## Verify the background check invitation has been sent

To access the _Job Application_, click on the candidate's name. Navigate to _Screening_ > Background Check History tab to view the invitation status.
<Frame>
  <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Sent Invite" src="/images/workday-background-check/workday_bc_job_application_submitted_bc_26.png" />
</Frame>


## View the candidate's background check results

To access the _Job Application_, click on the candidate's name. Navigate to _Screening_ > Background Check History tab to view the updated results.
