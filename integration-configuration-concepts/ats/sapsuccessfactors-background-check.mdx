<Warning>
  This guidance assumes that you have administrative privileges for your SAP SuccessFactors account.
</Warning>

## Associate background check to the Job Requisition

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Job Requisition">
    Navigate to specific _Job Requisition_, hover over the specific requisition title to open the Actions menu and then select `View or Edit Requisition`.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Go to Actions menu" src="/images/sapsuccessfactors/background-check/sap_sf_bc_recruiting_JR_hove_actions_32.png" />
    </Frame>

    Select the correct values for the _Account ID_ and _Package ID_ fields. Click on the `Save Changes` button.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Both fields are visible" src="/images/sapsuccessfactors/background-check/sap_sf_bc_job_req_bgi_package_fields_40.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>


## To Send the background check to the Candidate

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Talent Pipeline">
    Navigate to the specific _Job Requisition_ and click on Candidates. Select the candidate, then choose the `Move Candidate` option from the Take Action menu.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Go to Talent Pipeline" src="/images/sapsuccessfactors/background-check/sap_sf_bc_candidate_take_actions_33.png" />
    </Frame>

    Choose `Background Check` from the dropdown menu, then click `Apply Updates` to move the candidate forward.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Apply Updates" src="/images/sapsuccessfactors/background-check/sap_sf_bc_candidate_move_candidate_modal_34.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Send Background Check to Candidate">
    Navigate to _Background Check_, click on the `Initiate Background Check` option in the Take Action menu.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Initiate Background Check" src="/images/sapsuccessfactors/background-check/sap_sf_bc_initiate_bc_to_send_35.png" />
    </Frame>
    Click on the `Confirm` to send the Background Check. You'll get the success on the same modal.
  </Step>

  <Step title="How to view the requested background check?">
    The sent background check request will appear in the candidate's application under the Background Check portlet.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Background Check Request portlet" src="/images/sapsuccessfactors/background-check/sap_sf_bc_bc_request_portlet_36.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>


## To Check Result of the Background Check

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Background Check portlet">
    Go to the `Background Check` stage, The results will be available in the Background Check portlet within the candidate's application.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Check Result of the Background Check" src="/images/sapsuccessfactors/background-check/sap_sf_bc_result_updated_37.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>
