## Adding Assessment/Background test check to Candidate.
There are two type of assessments in SmartRecruiters - Candidate Assessments and Inline Assessments 

<Steps title = "Candidate Assessments">
  <Step title="Access Job Application">
    1. User logs into SmartRecruiters
    2. Navigates to **People**
    3. Selects a specific candidate profile

    <Frame>
      ![User Configuration Form](/images/smartrecruiters-assessments-background-checks/select-candidate.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Initiate Assessment/Background Check">
    From the candidate profile:
    
    1. Look for the assessment section
    2. Click **Order Assessment** button
    
    <Frame>
      ![Send Assessment Button](/images/smartrecruiters-assessments-background-checks/order-assessment-button.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Package">
    In the assessment form:
    
    1. **Select Assessment Partner**: Select your assessment partner
    2. **Select Package**: Choose from available test packages
    3. **Click Continue**: Click on continue button 
    
    <Frame>
      ![Package Selection Form](/images/smartrecruiters-assessments-background-checks/package-selection-form.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Send to Candidate">
    1. Review the test details and candidate information
    2. Click **Send** button
    3. Candidate receives email notification with test link and instructions

    <Frame>
      ![Package Selection Form](/images/smartrecruiters-assessments-background-checks/send-test-to-candidate.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>
