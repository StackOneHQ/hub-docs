## Enable the integration in your SmartRecruiters account
<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Navigate to `Settings` in the SmartRecruiters dashboard. On the home page, click your account avatar and select `Settings`.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Navigate to Settings" src="/images/smartrecruiters-assessments-background-checks/sandbox-settings.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open Apps & Integrations">
    Select `Apps & Integrations` under Settings.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Apps & Integrations" src="/images/smartrecruiters-assessments-background-checks/integration-page.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select the integration">
    Locate and click the target integration in the list.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Select Integration" src="/images/smartrecruiters-assessments-background-checks/select-integration.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review details & Connect">
    Review the integration details and click `Connect`.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="View Integration Details" src="/images/smartrecruiters-assessments-background-checks/integration-details.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Authorize integration">
    1. Review the requested permissions.
    2. Click the **Allow and Continue** button.

    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Enable Integration Connect Button" src="/images/smartrecruiters-assessments-background-checks/enable-integration.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Submit credentials">
    Enter the external trigger token and email in the form, then click the `Submit` button.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Input External Trigger Token" src="/images/smartrecruiters-assessments-background-checks/input-external-trigger-token.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm connection">
    After successful verification, a confirmation toast appears in the top‑right corner: `StackOne Assessments is connected`.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Integration Connected Successfully" src="/images/smartrecruiters-assessments-background-checks/integration-connected.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>


## Add an assessment / background check to a candidate

SmartRecruiters supports two assessment delivery modes: <b>Candidate Assessments</b> (manual per candidate) and <b>Inline Assessments</b> (auto‑triggered by workflow configuration).

### Candidate Assessments
<Steps>
  <Step title="Open candidate profile">
    1. Log in to SmartRecruiters.
    2. Navigate to **People**.
    3. Open the desired candidate profile.

    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Select Candidate" src="/images/smartrecruiters-assessments-background-checks/select-candidate.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Initiate assessment/background check">
    From the candidate profile:
    1. Scroll to the Assessments section.
    2. Click the **Order Assessment** button.
    
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Order Assessment Button" src="/images/smartrecruiters-assessments-background-checks/order-assessment-button.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select package">
    In the assessment modal:
    1. <b>Select Assessment Partner</b>: Choose the assessment vendor.
    2. <b>Select Package</b>: Pick a test package.
    3. Click **Continue**.
    
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Package Selection Form" src="/images/smartrecruiters-assessments-background-checks/package-selection-form.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Send to candidate">
    1. Review test details and candidate information.
    2. Click **Send**.
    3. The candidate receives an email with the test link and instructions.

    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Send Test to Candidate" src="/images/smartrecruiters-assessments-background-checks/send-test-to-candidate.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>


### Inline Assessments
Configure an inline assessment that auto‑triggers when a candidate is added to a job or moved into the assessment stage:
<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Click the profile icon and select `Settings`.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Access Admin Settings" src="/images/smartrecruiters-assessments-background-checks/access-admin-settings.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open Hiring Process">
    Under Settings, click `Hiring Process`.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Access Hiring Process" src="/images/smartrecruiters-assessments-background-checks/access-hiring-process.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select hiring process">
    Create a new hiring process or select the default one, then click the `Edit` icon.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Select Hiring Process" src="/images/smartrecruiters-assessments-background-checks/select-hiring-process-type.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Skills Test step">
    Click `Add Step` and choose `Skills Test`.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Add Skills Test Step" src="/images/smartrecruiters-assessments-background-checks/add-select-step.png" />
    </Frame>
    The step is added to the hiring process:
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Skills Test Step Added" src="/images/smartrecruiters-assessments-background-checks/selected-step.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save hiring process">
    Click `Save Hiring Process` to persist the changes.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Save Hiring Process" src="/images/smartrecruiters-assessments-background-checks/save-hiring-process.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add assessment entry">
    In the `Skills Test` step, open the menu and select `Assessments`.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Add Assessments" src="/images/smartrecruiters-assessments-background-checks/add-assessments.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure assessment">
    Click `Configure`.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Configure Assessment" src="/images/smartrecruiters-assessments-background-checks/configure-assessments.png" />
    </Frame>
    Select the assessment partner, assessment type, and package. Click `Save`.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Select Assessment Package" src="/images/smartrecruiters-assessments-background-checks/select-assessment-package-config.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enable auto‑trigger">
    Return to the `Config` page and enable the `Auto-triggered` toggle for the package.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Enable Auto Trigger" src="/images/smartrecruiters-assessments-background-checks/enable-auto-trigger.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add candidate">
    Click the `+` icon (top right) and choose `Add Candidate`.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Add Candidate" src="/images/smartrecruiters-assessments-background-checks/add-candidate.png" />
    </Frame>
  You can upload files or add a candidate manually. For this guide we follow the manual addition form path.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Manually Added Candidate" src="/images/smartrecruiters-assessments-background-checks/manually-added-candidate.png" />
    </Frame>
    Complete the form and click `Add Candidate`.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Candidate Form" src="/images/smartrecruiters-assessments-background-checks/candidate-form.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Move candidate to assessment stage">
    Open **People** and select the candidate.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Select Candidate for Stage" src="/images/smartrecruiters-assessments-background-checks/select-candidate-for-stage.png" />
    </Frame>
    Click `Move Forward` and select `Skills Test` under the `In Review` stage.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Select Assessment Stage" src="/images/smartrecruiters-assessments-background-checks/select-assessment-stage.png" />
    </Frame>
    A checkmark appears on `Skills Test` once the assessment order is sent.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Inline Stage Sent" src="/images/smartrecruiters-assessments-background-checks/inline-stage-sent.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>
