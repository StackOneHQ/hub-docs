## Add Candidate to the Job

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add a New Candidate within the Job">
    Navigate to the <i>Add Candidate</i> page to add a candidate and select the relevant job from the dropdown list.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Add New Candidate within the Job" src="/images/greenhouse-assessment/Add_Candidate_1.png" />
    </Frame>

    After adding a candidate, you will be directed to the candidate's profile page.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Candidate Profile Page" src="/images/greenhouse-assessment/Candidate_Profile_To_Move_1.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Move Candidate to the assessment or background check stage

<Steps>
  <Step title="Move Candidate to the assessment or background check stage">
    Click the `Move Stage` button in the top-right corner, then select `Your Assessment or Background Check Integration` to proceed.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Move Candidate to the Assessment or Background Check Stage" src="/images/greenhouse-assessment/Move_Candidate_2.png" />
    </Frame>

    Candidate will be moved to the assessment or background check stage.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Add New Candidate within the Job" src="/images/greenhouse-assessment/Moved_Candidate_3.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Send Webhook to the Candidate

<Steps>
  <Step title="Please choose "Test" from the dropdown menu to send the assessment or background check.">
    Click the `Send test to candidate` link to open a pop-up, allowing the recruiter to select which assessment or background check to send to the candidate.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Move Candidate to the assessment or background check Stage" src="/images/greenhouse-assessment/Select_Assessment_1.png" />
    </Frame>

    Notification will be sent to the candidate by clicking the `Send Test` button. You will receive confirmation at the same stage,
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Add New Candidate within the Job" src="/images/greenhouse-assessment/Sent_Assessment_Webhook_1.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## To check updated result of the assessment or background check invitation of the candidate

<Steps>
  <Step title="To see the updated results of assessment">
    Click on the candidate's name to view their application. You will see the updated results along with the assessment or background check integration stage from the previously sent webhook
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="View Assessment or Background Check Updated Results" src="/images/greenhouse-assessment/Check_Result.png" />
    </Frame>
    After the candidate has completed the assessment, click the `Go to test` link to view the results report.

  </Step>
</Steps>
