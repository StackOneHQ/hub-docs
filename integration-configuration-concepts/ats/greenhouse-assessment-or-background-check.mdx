## Send the assessment/background check invitation to the candidate

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add Assessment/Background Check stage with in the specific Job">
    Navigate to the <i>Job's</i> page to add/edit the Job via searching specific Job such as <i>Software Engineer</i>.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Go to Job Page" src="/images/greenhouse-assessment/Go_to_Job_Page_1.png" />
    </Frame>

    Click on the Job to edit & after scrolling down a bit and Click on the `Interview Plan` under the Job Setup.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Edit Job" src="/images/greenhouse-assessment/Go_to_Job_Edit_2.png" />
    </Frame>

    Click on the Add Stage button to add new stage from Dropdown list.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Edit Job Setup" src="/images/greenhouse-assessment/Go_to_Interview_Plan_1.png" />
    </Frame>

    You'll see the added stage within the Interview Plan.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Edit Job Setup" src="/images/greenhouse-assessment/Go_to_Interview_Plan_2.png" />
    </Frame>

    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Edit Job Setup" src="/images/greenhouse-assessment/Interview_Plan_Edited_3.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Add Candidate to the Job

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add a New Candidate within the Job">
    Navigate to the <i>Add Candidate's</i> page to add the Candidate and select specific same Job from the Dropdown list options.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Add New Candidate within the Job" src="/images/greenhouse-assessment/Add_Candidate_1.png" />
    </Frame>

    After adding Candidate, You'll directed to the Candidate's profile page.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Candidate Profile Page" src="/images/greenhouse-assessment/Candidate_Profile_To_Move_1.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Move Candidate to the assessment/background check stage

<Steps>
  <Step title="Move Candidate to the assessment/background check stage">
    Click on the `Move Stage` button at the top-right corner, and Select `Your Assessment/Background Check Integration` to move.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Move Candidate to the Assessment/Background Check Stage" src="/images/greenhouse-assessment/Move_Candidate_2.png" />
    </Frame>

    Candidate will be moved to the assessment/background check stage.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Add New Candidate within the Job" src="/images/greenhouse-assessment/Moved_Candidate_3.png.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Send Webhook to the Cnadidate

<Steps>
  <Step title="Move Candidate to the assessment/background check Stage">
    Click on the `Send test to candidate` link that will open the Pop-up and allow Recruiter to select assessment/background check that needs to be sent to the Candidate.
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Move Candidate to the assessment/background check Stage" src="/images/greenhouse-assessment/Select_Assessment_1.png" />
    </Frame>

    Notification will be sent to the Candidate by Clicking on the `Send Test` button. You'll get the confirmation on the same stage,
    <Frame>
      <img className='rounded-md' style={{ margin: '0 auto', border: '1px solid #efefef' }} alt="Add New Candidate within the Job" src="/images/greenhouse-assessment/Sent_Assessment_Webhook_1.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>
